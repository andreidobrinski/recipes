## Main Ingredients

<List>
  {[
    'Chickpeas - 1 can',
    'Tahina - 1 tbsp',
    'Olive Oil - 1 tbsp',
    'Water - 1 tbsp',
    'Garlic - 2 cloves chopped',
    'Onion - 1 tbsp chopped',
    'Lemon juice - 1 wedge squeezed',
  ]}
</List>

### Spices, to taste

<List>{['Salt', 'Pepper', 'Cumin', 'Paprika', 'Chili Powder', 'Cayenne']}</List>

### Garnish, to taste

<List>{['Sesame seeds', 'Roasted chickpeas', 'Olive oil', 'Spices']}</List>

## Steps

<List>
  {[
