Dough

Main Ingredients

  • Flour - 2 cups

  • Water - 1 cup

  • Yeast - 1/2 tsp

  • Salt - 1/2 tsp

  • Olive Oil - 1/8 cup (optional, for pizza dough)

Steps

  • Boil a small amount of water

  • Into a large bowl, add 1/3 cup of boiling water and 2/3 cup of cold water

  • Water should be just under 100°F, check with a thermometer

  • Add salt, yeast, olive oil to water and stir

  • Add flour and mix together

  • Cover with towel and let rise for 2 hours before using

Bake into bread or pizza.

Note: a Danish dough whisk works well for mixing dough.
