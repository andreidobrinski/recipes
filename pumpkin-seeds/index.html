Pumpkin Seeds

Main Ingredients

  • Pumpkin Seeds

  • Olive Oil

  • Garlic Salt

  • Cumin

  • Paprika

  • Chili Powder

  • Garam Masala

  • Cayenne

Steps

  • Rinse and dry pumpkin seeds

  • In a bowl, mix seeds with olive oil and spices

  • Bake at 400°f for 25 mins, checking periodically
